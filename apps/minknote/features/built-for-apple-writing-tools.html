Built for Apple Writing Tools

Because MinkNote is fully native to macOS, it automatically supports Apple Writing Tools. You can use system-level features to improve clarity, adjust tone, or rephrase text without leaving your note.

All features

MinkNote is designed to feel at home on macOS, which means it works naturally with Apple Writing Tools. You can highlight text and use system writing features to rewrite, proofread, or refine your words directly inside the app. There’s no separate editor and no special setup required.

Since these tools are built into the operating system, they behave exactly as you expect. Your text stays local, your workflow stays uninterrupted, and the experience remains consistent with other apps on your Mac.

This integration keeps writing simple and focused. You get the benefits of Apple’s system-wide tools while keeping your notes private, organised, and stored as real Markdown files on your disk.
