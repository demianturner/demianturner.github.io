Your Notes Forever

Open, Simple, and Built to Last

Leaving Apple Notes? Your writing can come with you. MinkNote uses plain Markdown files stored right on your Mac, so your notes stay readable, editable, and easy to organise over time.

All features

MinkNote is built around open, human-readable Markdown files. If you export your notes from Apple Notes, you can bring them into MinkNote and keep working without being tied to a closed system. Your notes live as real files on disk, so you can open, edit, or back them up anytime, even outside the app.

Apple Notes exports are handled thoughtfully. Filenames can be shown as note titles, and simple text notes still look clean and readable even if they contain no Markdown formatting.

This plain-text foundation keeps your work flexible and dependable. Whether you stay with MinkNote or choose a different tool later, your notes remain yours. No lock-in. No surprises. Just simple files designed to last.
