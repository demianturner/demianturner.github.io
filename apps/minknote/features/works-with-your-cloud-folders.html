Your Notes Forever
All features

MinkNote is fully compatible with cloud-synced folders. If you keep your notes in iCloud Drive, Dropbox, or another file-based sync service, everything works as expected. Your notes are simple Markdown files, so they sync reliably alongside your other documents.

There’s no custom syncing system and no background service to manage. You choose the folder, and your cloud provider handles the rest. This keeps your workflow familiar and transparent, while still giving you the flexibility to access your notes on multiple Macs.

Because everything is file-based, you can also move your project in or out of the cloud at any time. Your notes remain accessible, readable, and under your control.
