All features

MinkNote is designed for long-term ownership. Your notes live as regular Markdown-compatible files in folders you control, so your knowledge base is never trapped behind a vendor format.

You can use Finder, Git, backup tools, and sync providers without translation layers. This is especially important if your notes are part of real work: research archives, client documentation, writing pipelines, or personal knowledge systems that must stay readable for years.

The app gives you a strong editing and navigation experience, while your storage remains transparent, portable, and future-proof.
