MinkNote makes reorganising your work feel effortless. Just drag a note to a new folder or journal, and it moves instantly. There’s no need to worry about breaking links or losing files along the way.

Images and related media travel with the note automatically. The app updates file locations behind the scenes, keeping your structure clean and consistent on disk. You stay focused on organising your ideas, not managing file paths.

Because everything remains stored as real files in a transparent folder structure, you can trust that your content stays intact. Move things around as your thinking evolves. MinkNote handles the details, so your knowledge stays organised and easy to maintain.
