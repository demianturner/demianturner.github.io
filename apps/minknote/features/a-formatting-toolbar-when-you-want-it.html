A Formatting Toolbar When You Want It

MinkNote includes a simple formatting toolbar for quick access to common styles. You can keep it visible for convenience, or hide it to enjoy a clean, distraction-free writing space.

The formatting toolbar gives you fast access to headings, emphasis, lists, links, tables, and more. It’s designed to help you format content quickly without remembering every Markdown shortcut.

If you prefer a minimalist workspace, you can hide the toolbar at any time. Markdown remains fully supported, so you can continue typing naturally using keyboard shortcuts or plain syntax.

This flexibility lets you tailor the editor to your workflow. Keep the tools within reach when you want visual controls, or clear them away and focus entirely on your writing. Either way, your notes stay clean, structured, and ready to use anywhere.
