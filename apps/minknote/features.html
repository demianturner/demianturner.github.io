Features

Everything MinkNote can do, thoughtfully built for macOS power users.

Local-First File Ownership

MinkNote stores notes as standard text files in normal folders, not in a proprietary cloud container. You can inspect, back up, version, and move your files with familiar macOS tools.

Learn more

Work Across Projects with Ease

MinkNote lets you work with more than one project. Each project is simply a folder on your Mac, making it easy to separate different areas of your work and switch between them at any time.

Learn more

Organise Clearly with Journals and Folders

Journals group related notes together, while folders add another layer of organisation. This simple hierarchy keeps your work tidy, flexible, and easy to navigate as it grows.

Learn more

Two Simple Ways to Capture Your Ideas

MinkNote supports two clear note styles. Standard notes are for ideas that grow and change. Journal notes are for moments in time, automatically organised by date.

Learn more

Write Your Way: Markdown, Rich Text, or Both

MinkNote lets you write in Markdown or use a rich text editor, depending on how you like to work. You can even use Split View to type in Markdown and see it update instantly as beautifully rendered content.

Learn more

A Formatting Toolbar When You Want It

MinkNote includes a simple formatting toolbar for quick access to common styles. You can keep it visible for convenience, or hide it to enjoy a clean, distraction-free writing space.

Learn more

Powerful Tables, Made Simple

MinkNote gives you rich table controls directly inside your notes. Add or remove rows and columns, adjust alignment, and reshape your layout in seconds, all while keeping clean Markdown behind the scenes.

Learn more

Paste from the Web, Keep What Matters

When you paste from a web page, MinkNote keeps the useful structure like headings, lists, and links, while stripping away clutter. The result feels tidy and easy to read, without extra formatting noise.

Learn more

Images, Organised Automatically

Your notes keep simple inline references to those images, which means they remain easy to read, move, and use outside the app if needed.

Reorganise with Drag and Drop

You can move notes between folders and journals with simple drag and drop. When you do, MinkNote keeps everything together, including images and attachments, so nothing gets left behind.

Learn more

Organise with Tags and Favourites

Tags and favourites help you organise your notes in a way that feels natural. Add tags to connect related ideas across journals, and mark important notes as favourites so they're always close at hand.

Learn more

Manage Note Details with Frontmatter

Frontmatter is a small block of hidden information at the top of your note. MinkNote uses it to manage things like tags, pinning, and internal identifiers. Most of the time, you won't need to think about it at all.

Learn more

Find Anything, Instantly

MinkNote includes three distinct search tools, each designed for a specific job. You can search within the current note, filter notes inside a journal, or search across your entire project. It's fast, precise, and built to match how you think.

Learn more

Navigate Your Mac with the Keyboard

MinkNote is designed for people who prefer the keyboard. You can jump between journals, switch notes, and return to the editor without reaching for the mouse. The flow feels natural on macOS and helps you stay in the moment.

Learn more

Work Across Multiple Windows

Screenshot coming soon

MinkNote lets you open any note in its own window. This makes it easy to view two notes at once, copy information between them, or keep reference material visible while you write. Each window behaves just like the main app.

Learn more

Refined Appearance & Interface

MinkNote is designed to blend naturally into your Mac. You can tailor how notes appear in the list, choose whether counts are visible, and decide when front matter shows in Markdown mode. The editor layout can also be adjusted to match how you prefer to write.

Learn more

Save the Way You Prefer

By default, MinkNote saves your changes automatically, so your writing is always up to date. If you prefer more control, you can turn off auto-save and use ⌘S to save whenever you choose. The choice is yours.

Learn more

Works with Your Cloud Folders

You can store your MinkNote project in a cloud-synced folder such as iCloud Drive, Dropbox, or similar services. Because your notes are plain Markdown files, they sync just like any other document. There's no special setup required, and you stay in control of where your files live.

Learn more

Open, Simple, and Built to Last

Leaving Apple Notes? Your writing can come with you. MinkNote uses plain Markdown files stored right on your Mac, so your notes stay readable, editable, and easy to organise over time. There's no hidden database and no proprietary format locking things away.

Learn more

Built for Apple Writing Tools

Because MinkNote is fully native to macOS, it automatically supports Apple Writing Tools. You can use system-level features to improve clarity, adjust tone, or rephrase text without leaving your note. Everything feels integrated and consistent with the way writing works across your Mac.
