Privacy Policy

Effective: July 10, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. Which Bin is designed to operate without collecting or storing personal information on our servers.

What We Collect

  • Location Data: Used locally on your device to determine your postcode and look up your bin schedule. It’s never transmitted or stored externally.
  • Postcode: Stored locally for convenience. Not shared or uploaded.

What We Don’t Do

  • No account is required.
  • No personal data is collected or stored externally.
  • No data is shared with any third parties.
  • No tracking, ads, or analytics are used.

Your Control

You can delete the app at any time. All data is removed with it. We retain nothing.
